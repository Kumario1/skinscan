Is the catalog process repeatable?

The pipeline stages are repeatable. The complete autonomous loop is not built yet.

StageToday
DailyMed candidate extractionAutomated
Manufacturer evidence researchAgent judgment/manual capture
Schema validation and signingAutomated after review
Import and quarantineAutomated and deterministic
Coverage measurementAutomated
Next-batch selection and repetitionNot orchestrated

The next engineering unit is one resumable command that owns the state machine described in the loop reference. Its stopping condition must combine role/path coverage, evidence freshness, unmatched IDs, conflict rate, and quarantine rate.
