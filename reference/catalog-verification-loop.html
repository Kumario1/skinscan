Verified catalog loop

  1. Target: choose products from the largest coverage shortfall.
  2. Research: capture exact variant sources and hashes.
  3. Propose: assert only facts explicitly supported by each source.
  4. Review: reject conflicts, missing fields, or mismatched variants.
  5. Approve: sign with reviewer identity, type, and timestamp.
  6. Import: apply the overlay and emit quarantine reasons.
  7. Measure: regenerate completeness and quality metrics.
  8. Stop or repeat: stop only when coverage, freshness, and error targets pass.

Fail closed: unavailable evidence produces a rejection or proposed assertion—never a guessed approval.
