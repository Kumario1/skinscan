# Mission: Build a repeatable verified skincare catalog

## Why
Create an agent-operated process that can repeatedly turn legacy product rows
into trustworthy recommendation candidates without silently inventing facts.

## Success looks like
- An agent can select, research, approve, import, and audit successive batches.
- Every eligible field is traceable to exact product and regulatory evidence.
- Coverage and freshness targets determine when the catalog is good enough.

## Constraints
- Catalog approval may be performed by an identified agent.
- Clinical policy and release gates remain separate and require their own review.
- Conflicts, missing evidence, and discontinued variants must fail closed.

## Out of scope
- Diagnosing skin conditions or treating agent approval as clinical approval.
